Examples of New Product Development

New Generation Biosafety Cabinets with improved features

Challenge

Previous generation Biosafety cabinets lacked modern features, needed quality improvements, cost reductions and accessories

Solution

Localize most of the improvements from a 1500 BSC from China and address the need of the North American market customers via manufacturing this instrument locally

Innovation

A variety of improvements including quality improvement such as 1) Improved air flow via separate downflow and exhaust fans 2) Touchscreen convenience 3) Improved glass window mechanism with haptic feedback based positioning 4) Blower speed automatically reduced when front sash is opened also energy usage is reduced when the BSC is not in use 5) Programmable UV light for disinfection and reducing the risk of sample contamination.6) Ease of cleaning with SmartClean front window design and access to hard to reach areas. 7) Clean and safe routing through SmartPort access and ergonomic-centric accessories such as padded arm rest. Newer innovation based on flow control and optimization are under investigation for future patent filings.

Value

Enhanced aesthetic, ergonomic, safety, quality and performance values were acheived
