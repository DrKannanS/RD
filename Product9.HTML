Examples of New Product Development

New Generation Suppressor

Challenge

Previous generation suppressors had gasket materials that were prone to thermal damage and deformities. The suppressors were not designed for small particle packed columns-less than 5 micrometer particles. The suppressors were not resilient to operability under higher presssure conditions particularly when connecting secondary detectors at tandem

Solution

Hardware based sealing that improves the operational pressure and temperature resiliency. Eluent channel is packed with ion exchange particles to improve the flow dynamics and retain the efficiencies of early eluting peaks and shows improved performance when pursuing analysis with smaller particle packed columns and secondary detectors such as the UV and MS detection.

Innovation

Redesigned hardware for improved sealing and is a patented technology with a thin eluent channel packed with ion exchange resin and designed to improve operational static capacity, flow dynamics and maximize efficiences. R&D 100 🏅 Award Winner!

Value

Both internal company values by improving quality and assembly in the factory and external customer values such as improved performance (Improved peak efficiencies for early elutors, compatibility with smaller particle clad columns, Compatibility with higher temperature separation conditions) and more resiliency under customer workflow conditions.
