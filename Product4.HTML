Examples of New Product Development

Automation friendly Incubators

Key Contributions

Governance focus: New product automation architecture; Key differentiators; Collaboration with customers for agile development; FTO & IP filings; Regular Meetings for Visibility

Challenge

Legacy incubators cannot be easily integrated with a robot for developing automated cell growth harvesting setups. The door opening is not readily automatable adding to the process steps and labor during cell harvesting. Further a single user also struggles with opening the incubator doors when their hands are full when carrying the cell growth vessels.

Solution

Addition of a linear actuator was needed to facilitate opening of the door with good regard for safety for the user. The solution will aid automation through a trigger command to open the door for compatibility with robots and ease of use for standard customers.

Innovation

Actuator design integrated for remote triggered door opening to allow for facile integration with a robot. Also enabling ease of use in the lab by facilitating door opening through a wired foot rest based trigger so a single person can manage the workflow of inserting or removing GREX containers from the incubators during cell harvesting workflow. Scaleup of cell harvest is feasible in the present approach.

Value

The approach confers automation and ease of use to the user. Further safety is implicit in the present approach since hands free operation is feasible particularly when moving larger cell growth containers and in and out of the incubators. Collaboration with a customer Multiply Laboratories Inc. San Francisco who adopted the AXD into their cell harvesting protocols.
