Examples of New Product Development

New Generation Centrifugation with Green Cooling

Challenge

Previous generation floor model centrifuges used flourinated refrigerants for cooling purposes. Fluorinated gases, including hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs), perfluorocarbons (PFCs), and sulfur hexafluoride (SF6), have a high global warming potential (GWP). These regulations seek to limit the use, production, and release of these gases. Thus regulatory compliance in the EU and US was the key driver for this project.

Solution

Exploring alternate refrigerants which aim to reduce the emission of fluorinated greenhouse gases to mitigate their impact on climate change with low GWP became the driver for this project. CO2 with a GWP of 1 became an attractive solution.

Innovation

Novel refrigeration concept with CO2. Filed several patents covering the general architechture and the safety concept.

Value

Green cooling technology with CO2 brings several values over legacy units such as lower Global Warming Potential GWP=1, Reduced energy consumption, Lighter weight modules making it cheaper to ship with less carbon footprint, lower operational noise levels and finally a non-flammable refrigerant conferring operational safety and suitable for air freight. World's first green centrifuge with a non-flammable refrigerant that is fluoride free!
